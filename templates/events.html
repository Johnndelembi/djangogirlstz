Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions
about our workshops and organization.

What is the purpose

Our purpose is to empower and help women by organizing free, one-day programming workshops. We provide tools, resources, and support to make technology more approachable.

Who can Attend

Our workshops are open to all women who are interested in learning programming, regardless of their previous experience or background.

How to get Involved

You can get involved by volunteering with us, contributing resources, or supporting our mission through donations.

Are there any fees for the workshops?

Yes, all our workshops are free of charge. We believe in making technology education accessible to everyone.

How to Apply

To apply for our workshops, simply fill out the application form on our website. Spaces are limited, so apply early!
