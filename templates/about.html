About Django Girls

Django Girls is a non-profit organization and a community that empowers women to organize free, one-day programming workshops by providing tools, resources, and support. We are a volunteer-run organization with hundreds of people contributing to bring more amazing women into the world of technology.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to foster a deep passion for software development through targeted outreach, empowering women to embrace technical skills, and increasing participation in tech-related fields.

Vision

Our vision is to create an inclusive tech ecosystem where women are empowered to lead, innovate, and shape the future of technology, significantly increasing their representation and impact in tech-related fields in Africa and the world.
